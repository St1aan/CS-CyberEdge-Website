What It Does

A security breach demands an immediate, expert response. Our Incident Response service puts certified professionals directly into your environment — remotely or on-site — to take decisive action. We contain the attack, stop the bleeding, and begin the forensic work needed to understand exactly what happened.

Beyond the technical response, we help you navigate the difficult decisions that come with a breach: what to tell the board, when to notify regulators, and how to communicate with affected customers. We stay with you from the moment of engagement through full recovery.

Containment

Digital Forensics

Deep-dive investigation to determine the attack vector, dwell time, and full scope.

Eradication

Complete removal of the attacker's foothold, malware, and persistence mechanisms.

Guidance on breach notification obligations to regulators and affected parties.
