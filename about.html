// OUR STORY

Built to Close the Gap

CS CyberEdge was founded by cybersecurity professionals holding industry-leading certifications — including CISSP, CISM, OSCP, and CCSKv5 — with more than a decade of combined experience across the financial and technology sectors.

Built on a straightforward conviction — that every organization deserves access to effective cyber protection, not just large enterprises with massive security budgets — the company was created to level the playing field.

After years working alongside major corporations and leading cybersecurity consulting firms, our founders witnessed firsthand the depth of investment large organizations make to defend against evolving threats. At the same time, small to medium enterprises were routinely left exposed — hindered by limited resources, skills shortages, and constrained budgets.

CS CyberEdge was built to close that gap.

// OUR MISSION

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide practical, proactive cybersecurity solutions that help organizations strengthen their first line of defense before an attack occurs. Cybercriminals frequently target businesses through basic and preventable threat vectors. By helping our clients close the front door, we make it significantly harder for attackers to gain a foothold — compelling them to move on to easier targets.
