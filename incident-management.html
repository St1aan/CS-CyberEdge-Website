What It Does

Security incidents rarely fail because of a lack of tools — they fail because of a lack of process. Our Incident Management service provides the structured framework your team needs to coordinate effectively under pressure. We manage the full incident lifecycle, from the moment an alert fires to the final post-incident report.

We work alongside your team to classify severity, assign ownership, drive escalations, maintain a real-time incident timeline, and ensure the right stakeholders are informed at every stage. Nothing is assumed and nothing is left undocumented.

Triage & Classification

Rapid severity rating and initial scoping to prioritise your response effort.

Escalation Workflows

Defined escalation paths ensure the right people are looped in at the right time.

Stakeholder Comms

Clear, timely communication to leadership, legal, and affected parties.

Incident Timeline

Continuous documentation of actions and findings throughout the incident.

Key Features

Structured Incident Lifecycle

Defined playbooks for every incident phase — detection, containment, eradication, recovery, and lessons learned — so your team always knows what to do next.

Real-Time Coordination

Centralised incident command keeps all responders aligned, prevents duplicated effort, and maintains momentum through every phase of the response.

Post-Incident Reporting

Comprehensive after-action reports covering the full timeline, root cause analysis, business impact assessment, and prioritised remediation recommendations.
