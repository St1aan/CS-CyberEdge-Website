What It Does

SecureScore is a lightweight, expert-designed cyber risk assessment built specifically for SMBs that don't have an in-house security team. Clients work through a structured questionnaire covering the three pillars of cyber security — people, process, and technology.

The service produces a numeric risk score with a breakdown by domain, and a prioritised remediation roadmap tailored to your organisation's size, sector, and budget. The resulting report is formatted to satisfy insurer due-diligence requests or supply-chain questionnaires from larger clients.

People

Security awareness, training, access controls, and phishing readiness.

Process

Policies, incident response plans, patch management, and vendor reviews.

Technology

Endpoint protection, backups, MFA, network segmentation, and cloud security.

Formal Report

Insurer-ready and client-facing format to satisfy due-diligence requirements.

How It Works

Three simple steps from sign-up to a clear picture of your cyber risk.

1

Answer the Questionnaire

Work through a structured set of questions designed by certified security professionals. Takes 30–60 minutes. No technical knowledge required.

2

Receive Your Risk Score

Get a scored breakdown across people, process, and technology — benchmarked against similar-sized organisations in your sector.

3

Act on Your Roadmap

Receive a prioritised remediation roadmap with effort estimates and impact ratings. Know exactly what to fix first and why.
