What It Does

DomainWatcher uses advanced fuzzy-matching algorithms to identify domains that are visually or phonetically similar to yours. These "lookalike" domains are the primary vehicle for phishing and brand abuse.

We monitor global registrar feeds and DNS changes to alert you as soon as a suspicious domain is registered, giving you a head start on takedown procedures.
