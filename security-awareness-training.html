HUMAN RISK

What It Does

The vast majority of successful cyberattacks begin with a human mistake — a clicked link, a shared credential, an opened attachment. Our Security Awareness Training service addresses this directly by turning your workforce from a vulnerability into a line of defence.

We run realistic, organisation-specific phishing simulations to benchmark your current risk exposure, then deliver targeted training to the individuals who need it most. Progress is tracked over time so you can demonstrate measurable improvement to leadership, insurers, and auditors.

Phishing Simulations

Realistic, safe phishing campaigns that benchmark click rates and credential submission across your organisation.

Targeted Training Modules

Short, role-appropriate training delivered automatically to employees who need it after a simulation.

Risk Reporting

Department-level and individual risk scores that identify your highest-risk users and track improvement over time.

Executive Briefings

Clear summaries of human risk posture for leadership, framed in business impact terms, not technical jargon.

Key Features

Realistic Phishing Scenarios

Simulations are tailored to mimic the attack styles most relevant to your industry — including business email compromise, credential harvesting, and invoice fraud — so results reflect real-world risk.

Measurable Behaviour Change

We track click rates, reporting rates, and training completion over time, giving you the data to demonstrate a reduction in human risk to your board, cyber insurer, or compliance auditor.

Recurring Programme Design

Security awareness is not a one-time event. We design a rolling annual programme with varied scenarios, seasonal themes, and refresher modules to keep vigilance high year-round.
