What It Does

SurfaceWatch is a continuous attack surface management platform built for organisations that need enterprise-grade visibility without a dedicated security team. You register your software stack, network assets, and suppliers — SurfaceWatch does the rest.

It cross-references your assets against the NIST NVD and CISA Known Exploited Vulnerabilities (KEV) catalogue in real time, surfacing the CVEs that are most dangerous to your specific environment and mapping your exposure to relevant MITRE ATT&CK threat actors.

Asset Inventory

Track software, network assets, and third-party suppliers in one place.

CVE & KEV Matching

Real-time cross-referencing against NVD and CISA KEV databases.

Exposure Monitoring

Identify publicly exposed services and assess their risk level.

Supplier Risk

Monitor third-party vendors and SaaS providers for breach activity.

Key Features

Live Risk Score

A continuously updated aggregate risk score that gives you an at-a-glance view of your overall security posture, broken down by severity.

MITRE ATT&CK Mapping

Automatically map your detected vulnerabilities and exposures to relevant threat actors, so you know who is most likely to target you and how.

Prioritised Alerts

Not all CVEs are equal. SurfaceWatch prioritises alerts by exploitability, CISA KEV status, and exposure so your team focuses on what matters most.

Everything in One Platform

30-day trend charts across all severity levels.
